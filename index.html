About Me

My introduction

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Arnab Mondal, a Bachelor of Computer Applications undergraduate with a strong foundation in Server-side Java Development. With over a year of practical experience with Java/JVM ecosystem, I have developed expertise in creating high-performance, scalable, distributed and secure back-end solutions using Java/JVM technologies. My journey in software development has led me to successfully complete a variety of projects, including web-based messaging applications, real-time chat systems, and comprehensive management systems. My skill set includes proficiency in Java, Spring Boot, Hibernate, Docker, Linux, and AWS, as well as database management with both SQL and NoSQL technologies. I am passionate about continuous learning and staying updated with the latest industry trends and best practices. My technical interests extend to front-end technologies, desktop and mobile application development, cybersecurity, and low level programming making me a versatile and well-rounded developer ready to tackle complex challenges. In addition to my technical pursuits, I enjoy exploring electronics and technology, traveling to mountainous regions, and listening to music. I am enthusiastic about the potential of technology to bring ideas to life and am eager to contribute my skills and creativity to impactful projects. Feel free to explore my projects and certifications. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
